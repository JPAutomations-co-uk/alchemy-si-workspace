## Positioning

- **Core differentiator:** Alchemy designs systems from the user experience backward — "effortless operation is key." Not a box-shifter, not a cable-and-go installer. Systems are integrated cleanly, branded, and built to be reliable for years.
- **Against competitors:** Most AV integrators prioritise hardware specs over user experience. Alchemy prioritises the end-user — "uncomplicated and intuitive" is the design standard.
- **Against DIY / cheap installs:** One failed video call in front of a client costs more than the installation. Reliable systems pay for themselves.
- **Certifications:** CHAS, ISCVE member, Avixa, electrotechnical certified

### Common Objections

**"We already have a supplier / we use [X] for AV"**
→ What's the pain with them? Most clients who switch to Alchemy do so after a system that wasn't right the first time. Ask what the current system does well, what it doesn't. Position as the alternative for when the stakes are higher.

**"We can get it cheaper elsewhere"**
→ You can. The difference is what happens 12 months in — when the system fails before an important meeting, or when end-users refuse to use it because it's too complicated. Alchemy designs for zero-friction daily use. That's where the value is.

**"We're not ready yet / project is 6 months away"**
→ Perfect time to get involved — AV infrastructure is easiest and cheapest to integrate at first-fix. Getting Alchemy involved early means the system design is baked into the build, not bolted on at the end.

**"Can you just supply the hardware?"**
→ We can specify and supply, but the real value is in the design and integration. A system that's been properly designed and installed will outperform self-installed hardware from day one.
