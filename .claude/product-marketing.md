## Product / Service

**Name:** Alchemy System Integration
**Category:** AV & Smart Building Systems Integration (B2B, commercial)
**Core promise:** AV systems designed around how people actually work — intuitive, reliable, zero-friction.

---

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Primary:**
- Architects and main contractors specifying AV on commercial projects
- Facilities managers and office managers at mid-to-large businesses
- Hospitality operators (hotels, restaurants, bars, event venues)
- Commercial fit-out companies

**Secondary:**
- Schools, colleges, universities
- Worship venues and churches
- Theatre and performance venues

**Decision-maker profile:**
- Title: Director, MD, Facilities Manager, Project Manager, Office Manager
- Region: Birmingham / West Midlands (primary), UK-wide for larger projects
- Budget authority: Yes — or directly influencing whoever has it
- Trigger: New build, office refurbishment, AV upgrade, failed existing system

---

## Key Differentiators

1. **Design-led, not spec-led** — every system is designed from the user experience backward
2. **Discreet integration** — no ugly cabling, no black boxes visible, systems blend into the space
3. **End-to-end service** — from first consultation through to handover training and ongoing support
4. **Certifications** — CHAS, ISCVE, Avixa, electrotechnical — credible to specifiers and procurement teams
5. **Trusted brands** — Biamp, Q-SYS, Sennheiser, Sony, Panasonic, Epson, Denon
